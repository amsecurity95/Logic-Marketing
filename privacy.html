Privacy Policy

Effective April 2026

1. Who we are

Logic Marketing ("Logic", "we", "our") is a marketing studio with offices in Illinois, USA and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. This policy explains what we collect when you visit our website or hire us for a project, and what we do with it.

2. What we collect

  • Information you give us: name, email, phone, company, and any project details you share through our forms or by email.
  • Information collected automatically: IP address, browser, device, pages viewed, and approximate location, via standard analytics.
  • Cookies: we use essential cookies to make the site work and basic analytics cookies to understand how it's used. We don't use advertising trackers.

3. How we use it

  • To respond to inquiries and deliver the work you've engaged us for.
  • To improve the website and our services.
  • To send occasional updates — only if you've opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time.

4. Sharing

We don't sell your data. We share it only with vetted service providers (hosting, email, analytics) under confidentiality agreements, and only what they need to do their job. We may disclose information when required by law.

5. Retention

We keep project records for as long as we have an active relationship with you, plus the period required for tax, legal, and accounting purposes. After that, we delete or anonymize them.

6. Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, export, or delete the personal data we hold about you. Email info@logicmarketing.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, access controls) to protect your information. No method is 100% secure, but we treat your data like we'd want ours treated.

8. Children

Our services are not directed to anyone under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

If we update this policy, we'll change the "Effective" date at the top. Material changes will be announced on the homepage.
